India's Economic Growth Hits New High

India's economic growth is experiencing a period of high performance, with the latest reports indicating a strong recovery and growth, particularly in the fourth quarter. Tech Giants Invest in India's Startup Ecosystem

The Indian startup ecosystem is experiencing significant investment from tech giants, fueling growth and innovation. India has become the world's third-largest startup hub, with over 1.57 lakh startups recognized by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT). This ecosystem is characterized by a vibrant network of entrepreneurs, investors, incubators, and supporting entities.
